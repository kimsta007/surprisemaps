Need a little help?
The maps show vaccination rates of counties in the US.

The legend to the right of the maps shows the range each county falls within, given the color encoding. Clicking the legend color codings will show you a cluster of counties that belong to a particular range.

If you hover over the map, a tooltip will pop-up. The tooltip also shows the vaccination rate and surprise value for each county.

The Surprise value is a measure of how accurate the vaccination rate is given each counties population density.

We will ask you to use this map to give feedback on the task, based on how high or low performing counties are in terms of vaccination rates.
